INSIDE THE BRAND

Let’s tell you a little about us.

Tedprime Hub is a globally connected organization with its framework to usher in the new revolution where education becomes the main underlying factor in the new world and ecosystem that is engulfed with technology and innovation.

We believe in strategic partnership that promote required synergy for development of digitally driven workforce. Our team has conducted trainings and capacity development on education, technology, and standard practices in partnership with Microsoft, CISCO, and the Nigeria Computer Society.

As part of the diplomatic expertise of the co-founders, they have served as technical support team for Nigerian Students at international engagements such as German African Media Camp, Pan African Youth Leadership Programme of the US Government, and World Largest Lesson on SDGs.
